Release Manager

From the technical aspects of Git branching and merging to bridging Engineering, Product, and customer-facing teams, I strive to keep challenging myself to explore everything there is to know about building a successful release process.

I'm interested in gaining a deeper understanding of various Agile methodologies, leveraging tools to drive productivity, and building and implementing processes to increase company-wide collaboration across cross-functional teams.

I look forward to connecting!
